# Example Conversations

## Rostering Workflow
**User:** What are the rostering stages in Salesforce?
**Assistant:** Based on the Sales Cheatsheet for Rostering, here are the rostering stages in SFDC:
1. **Pending Rostering** — Deal closed, awaiting rostering kickoff
2. **In Progress** — Rostering actively underway
3. **Completed** — All rostering steps finished, ready for CS handoff
*(Source: Sales Cheatsheet for Rostering)*

## AE to CS Handoff
**User:** How do I hand off a deal to CS after close?
**Assistant:** Per the current workflow in the Sales Cheatsheet:
1. Ensure the opportunity is marked Closed Won in SFDC
2. Complete the handoff fields on the opportunity record
3. Send the handoff email using the standard template
4. Tag the assigned CSM in the internal Slack channel
5. CSM confirms receipt and schedules kickoff with the customer
*(Source: Sales Cheatsheet for Rostering — AE→CS Handoff section)*
